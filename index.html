Our research group focuses on problems (both industrial and fundamental in nature) pertaining to field of PROCESS METALLURGY. We are actively engaged in various inter-disciplinary areas of research like fluid flow modeling in packed beds, flow of granular materials, CO2 sequestration, spray deposition of molten metal, salt-extraction process, manufacturing of Boron Carbide and Silicon Carbide etc.
